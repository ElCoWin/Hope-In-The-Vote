What Are Agenda 47 and Project 2025?

Agenda 47: Donald Trump’s policy platform for a potential second term, focusing on issues like immigration, national security, energy independence, and economic nationalism. It reflects Trump's vision for a stronger, more self-reliant America.

Project 2025: Created by the Heritage Foundation and other conservative think tanks, Project 2025 is a detailed policy blueprint intended to guide a conservative administration in reshaping the federal government. It includes strategies for restructuring federal agencies, placing conservative personnel in key positions, and implementing conservative policies across the board.

Impact Statements

Agenda 47

Who Benefits: Conservative voters, traditionalists, corporations, and the wealthy.

Who Doesn't: Immigrants, minority communities, LGBTQ+ individuals, and low-income families.

Project 2025

Who Benefits: Conservative ideologues, political appointees in key roles.

Who Doesn't: Federal employees, marginalized groups, environmental advocates.
